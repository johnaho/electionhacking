How to write nesCE.cfg of keybind.

Usage.

Bind  [Command]  [VirtualKey]

   [Command] is Binding to [VirtualKey].


[Command]

KEY_START		Pad START  button.
KEY_SELECT		Pad SELECT button.
KEY_P1_A		Pad A      button.
KEY_P1_B		Pad B      button.
KEY_P1_TA		Turbo SW for A button.
KEY_P1_TB		Turbo SW for B button.
KEY_P1_UP		Pad UP     button.
KEY_P1_DOWN		Pad DOWN   button.
KEY_P1_LEFT		Pad LEFT   button.
KEY_P1_RIGHT	Pad RIGHT  button.
QUIT			Quit game.
KEY_RESET		Reset button.

STATE_SELECT_1	Stateslot selection slot1.
STATE_SELECT_2	Stateslot selection slot2.
STATE_SELECT_3	Stateslot selection slot3.
STATE_SELECT_4	Stateslot selection slot4.
STATE_SELECT_5	Stateslot selection slot5.
STATE_SELECT_6	Stateslot selection slot6.
STATE_SELECT_7	Stateslot selection slot7.
STATE_SELECT_8	Stateslot selection slot8.
STATE_SELECT_9	Stateslot selection slot9.
STATE_SELECT_0	Stateslot selection slot0.
STATE_SAVE		State Saving.
STATE_LOAD		State Loding.

DISK_EJECT 		Disk eject button.
DISK_INSERT		Disk inserting.
DISK_SELECT		Disk side Selecting.
